Taste of the Home

The Hungry Harmony specializes in delicious food featuring fresh ingredients and masterful preparation by the hungry harmony culinary team. Whether you’re ordering a multi-course meal or grabbing a drink and burger at the bar, the hungry harmony lively, casual yet upscale atmosphere makes it perfect for dining with friends, family, clients and business associates.

OUR DEALS

BEVERAGES

Beverages have been an integral part of human culture for centuries, offering not just hydration but also pleasure, indulgence, and even cultural significance.

Beverages quench our thirst, connect cultures, and offer a delightful escape from the ordinary.

STARTERS

Starter foods are small, savory dishes served before the main course of a meal. They’re designed to whet the appetite and prepare the palate for the flavors to come. These appetizers can be hot or cold, simple or elaborate, and incorporate a wide variety of ingredients. From antipasto platters with pepperoni and provolone to buffalo chicken dip, there’s something for every taste.

TANDOORI

Chicken Tandoori is a mouthwatering Indian dish where marinated chicken is cooked in a tandoor (clay oven).

The result? Juicy, smoky, and flavorful chicken with a vibrant red hue. It’s a must-try for food enthusiasts!

DESSERTS

Dessert is the delightful finale of a meal, tempting our taste buds with sweetness and variety.

VEG THALI

A Veg Thali is a delightful spread of various dishes, showcasing the rich culinary heritage of different Indian regions.
